Frequently Asked Questions

What is ELYSIUM?
ELYSIUM is an innovative token that combines the features of fungible and non-fungible tokens. It introduces a unique blend, allowing fractionalized ownership with each token linked to a non-fungible token (NFT).
How do I get ELYSIUM tokens?
To acquire ELYSIUM tokens, participate in the initial distribution during the Genesis phase. Stay engaged with the community for updates on airdrops, partnerships, and potential exchanges listing ELYSIUM.
What makes ELYSIUM different?
ELYSIUM stands out by offering a seamless fusion of fungibility and uniqueness. It enables fractionalized trading while linking each fraction to an NFT. This innovative approach redefines token standards, providing a dynamic and engaging user experience.
How can I be involved in shaping ELYSIUM's future?
Engage with the ELYSIUM community on official channels. Provide feedback, participate in governance decisions during the Fusion phase, and stay informed about upcoming proposals. Your involvement is crucial in shaping the evolution of ELYSIUM.
